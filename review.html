Review

"A fine dining experience..."

The Onion

You could hardly find a finer Chinese restaurant in China itself It's not often that I enjoy Chinese cuisine. Usually Chinese restaurants completely lack authenticity. They claim to be completely authentic while almost exclusively selling food you would find in your local chipper. But the Man Fu Palace is different. You could hardly find finer Chinese restaurant in China itself. Their menu is packed with traditional Chinese dishes one would expect to find on the menu; Szechuan Beef, Crab Rangoon, Chicken Chow Mein and what I tried - the Chicken Egg Fu Young. While I didn't taste much else, I can say for certain that the chefs here are of the highest standard and clearly have years of experience in this field.

But their chefs aren't the only members of staff that are of a high standard. The waiters and waitresses are also very professional. They are kind, warm and inviting. They aren't the dead-inside, fake-smile wearing waiters you'll find elsewhere. Oh no, they seem genuinely kind and are always willing to have a discussion. They really do seem to enjoy their work. But they're not just friendly faces. They're quick and good at what they do. You're never left sitting hungry waiting to get the attention of a nearby waiter. They come to you, and take care of you through your entire meal.

the finest cuisine and best service... In conclusion I would definitely recommend the Man Fu Palace. It's easily the finest cuisine and best service that can be found in Dublin City. The joined with incredibly generous prices makes it the best dining experience in Dublin. So why not consider the Man Fu Palace when next scheduling a business meeting or having a day out with the family?
