Welcome to the Man Fu Palace

Welcome

The Man Fu Palace opened its doors in 1995. Since then we have offered our clients everything people look for in a Chinese restaurant: a widely varied menu, delicious and authentic Chinese cuisine in generous portions and unrivalled customer service. Better still, we offer all of this at fantastic prices.

We offer distinctive cuisine in a friendly setting. Our restaurant, which is loacted in the heart of Dublin City, enjoys a reputation for impeccable service, elegant Asian-style decor, and exquisite Chinese food. Dining at our restaurant is like visiting China without leaving your hometown - an exciting blend of authentic Chinese food, culture, and atmosphere awaits. When it comes to ingredients we always strive to deliver the freshest, highest quality produce. Our ingredients are delivered daily and contain a mix of locally bought produce and authentic Chinese produce imported straight from China. With these high quality ingredients our talented chefs, many of whom have several decades of experience, cook delicious and authentic Chinese cuisine.

As a family-owned-and-operated restaurant, the Man Fu Palace takes great pride in creating scrumptious Chinese dishes for you and your family. From the garnishes on our plates to the pictures on the wall, we replicate the Chinese experience in every detail. Whether you come for a quiet dinner for two or gather your entire family for an exciting Chinese food adventure, a memorable experience awaits you at the Man Fu Palace.

We are open daily for lunch and dinner beginning at 11:00 am. Reservations are recommended. Plan your office party or family celebration in our newly expanded party room. If you are looking for a fun, high quality Chinese restaurant in Dublin City, look no further than the Man Fu Palace! So why not stop by some time for a delicious Chinese meal? Whether it's a business lunch, romantic date or a family meal out, there's no finer place to fine in all Dubline city than the Man Fu Palace!
